About Product Owner in Test™

Empowering software teams to explore, validate, and elevate quality with confidence and curiosity.

Our Mission

At Product Owner in Test™, we believe great software isn't just built — it's understood. We are committed to empowering people and teams to deeply comprehend the software they test, through hands-on training, practical certifications, and meaningful mentorship.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for quality and curiosity, Product Owner in Test™ began with a simple realization: software testing deserves more than scripted checks — it deserves exploration. We envision a world where testers are leaders of quality, not gatekeepers of pass/fail.

What We Offer

  • Training programs rooted in real-world product exploration
  • Hands-on certifications that validate skill, not just theory
  • Mentorship and community meetups to inspire growth

Join Us

Whether you're starting your journey or mastering your craft, Product Owner in Test™ welcomes you to a new era of software quality ownership. Let's explore together.
